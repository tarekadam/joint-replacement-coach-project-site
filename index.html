Meet Your Wellness Coach

Physiotherapist
& Joint Replacement Expert

I have been a physiotherapist for over 12 years and have worked in all areas of arthritis and joint replacement care. My experience in joint replacements spans from one-on-one physiotherapy to triaging wait-lists, to dealing with surgeons, and even healthcare administration. Together we will focus on your pre & post operative needs:

  • check

    preparing for surgery

  • check

    weight management

  • check

    physiotherapy & recovery

Your preparation for surgery and the road through recovery can be challenging. Simple things, like getting out of bed, can be a struggle if you don't have guidance. The care your receive both pre & post operation is going to be critical to your quality of life. So let's make the most of your rehabilitation both at home, and in your chosen physiotherapy clinic. Together we will create priorities, reduce your stress, and make the most of this life changing surgery!
